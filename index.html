NO ACCOUNT LOCAL DATA NO ADS NO AD TRACKING USER EXPORTS ONLY Sections 01 Overview 02 No Account / Ads / Profiling 03 Local-Only User Data 04 Camera, Photos, Files, OCR 05 Logbook and Exports 06 Part Number Notes 07 Notifications 08 Premium / Purchases 09 Data Sharing 10 Data Deletion 11 Children 12 Changes 13 Contact 01 Overview A32X AircraftLine, formerly prepared as A32X Line, is a standalone aviation maintenance reference, study, and training-support application for A319/A320/A321 line-maintenance workflows. This privacy policy explains what information is processed, where it is stored, and how the app uses Android permissions. 02 No Account, No Advertising, No User Profiling A32X AircraftLine does not require a user account. The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party advertising analytics SDKs, Crashlytics, Sentry, Mixpanel, AdMob, or similar user-profiling services. The app does not automatically collect your name, email address, location, contacts, advertising ID, or advertising profile data. Local OCR components may process technical diagnostics such as device/app information, performance metrics, events, errors, or identifiers according to the provider's SDK behavior and the store Data Safety disclosures. 03 Local-Only User Data The app stores user-created data locally on the device. This may include: app preferences such as language, dark mode, display size, and units; favorites and recent items; personal Part Number notes; local Logbook entries and imported preview records; personal documents, images, scans, and PDFs added by the user; damage/report notes and photos created by the user; contribution notes or field notes prepared by the user; local reminder settings. This data is not automatically uploaded by A32X AircraftLine. 04 Camera, Photos, Files, and OCR A32X AircraftLine may request camera or photo/media access when the user chooses a feature that needs it, for example: taking or selecting photos for personal documents; creating reports or scans; importing personal Part Number notes from a notebook photo; OCR processing of user-selected images/PDF previews where supported. OCR is intended to run locally on the device. The app does not automatically send photos or OCR content to a cloud service. The app does not scan the user's gallery in the background. 05 Logbook and Exports The Logbook module is for personal user-entered or user-imported experience records. It is a formatting and organization aid only. When the user exports PDF or CSV files, those files may contain personal experience records such as dates, aircraft registration, work descriptions, ATA references, hours, privileges, task/activity marks, and record references. Exports are user-initiated. If the user shares an export through email, messaging, cloud storage, or another app, that external app controls the transfer according to its own privacy policy. 06 Part Number Personal Notes Personal Part Number notes are stored separately from the app's built-in reference data. OCR-detected Part Number candidates are not saved automatically. The user must review, correct, and confirm entries before saving. Personal PN notes are user notes only and are not approved or validated technical data. 07 Notifications If enabled by the user, A32X AircraftLine may use local notifications for reminders. Notifications are local reminders only. They are not dispatch status, MEL approval, operational authorization, maintenance release, or airworthiness decisions. The app may request permission to restore local reminders after device restart. 08 Premium / Purchases If premium features are offered through Google Play, payments and purchase processing are handled by Google Play according to Google's own terms and privacy policy. A32X AircraftLine does not process payment card details directly. If store billing is added, the app may store local entitlement status needed to unlock purchased features. 09 Data Sharing A32X AircraftLine does not automatically sell, share, or upload user data. User data leaves the device only when the user intentionally exports, shares, emails, backs up, or transfers it through another app or service. 10 Data Deletion Users can delete local user-created records from the relevant app features where deletion is available. Uninstalling the app may remove local app data from the device, depending on Android and device backup behavior. Global cross-device backup/restore is not guaranteed unless a dedicated backup/restore feature is later implemented and clearly described. 11 Children A32X AircraftLine is intended for aviation maintenance professionals, trainees, and adult users interested in A320-family maintenance reference workflows. It is not directed at children. 12 Changes This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The latest version should be available from the Google Play listing and/or inside the app. 13 Contact For privacy questions, contact: aircraftline.support@gmail.com A32X AircraftLine is a reference, study, and training-support app only. It is not approved maintenance data and must not be used for operational, dispatch, maintenance release, certification, airworthiness, or GO/NO-GO decisions. Always verify with current approved operator/company/manufacturer documentation.
